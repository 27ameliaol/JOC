The Initial Insult

I am currently reading "The Inital Insult". This is what I think of it so far

Right now the book is pretty slow, but I was told it was a thriller. It is about two girls that have to run around their town from a panther. It is supposed to be a book that will keep you on the edge of your seat, which I enjoy. I hope this will be a book that causes you to keep flipping pages. I will update soon.
